# Legal

Legal templates, policies, and agreement frameworks.

---

## Purpose

Version-controlled legal templates and policies. **Not for signed documents or confidential agreements.**

---

## Structure

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `templates/` | Reusable agreement templates |
| `policies/` | Company policies (privacy, security, etc.) |
| `compliance/` | Regulatory compliance documentation |

---

## Templates

Standard agreement templates for common scenarios:
- NDA (Mutual and One-Way)
- Contractor Agreement
- Employment Offer Letter
- Service Agreement
- Data Processing Agreement

**Note:** Templates are starting points. Always review with legal counsel before use.

---

## Policies

Company policies that apply to operations:
- Privacy Policy
- Security Policy
- Acceptable Use Policy
- Data Retention Policy

---

## Compliance

Documentation supporting regulatory compliance:
- GDPR compliance documentation
- SOC 2 evidence templates
- Security questionnaire responses

---

## What Belongs Here

✅ Agreement templates (unsigned)
✅ Policy documents
✅ Compliance documentation templates
✅ Legal process documentation

---

## What Does NOT Belong Here

❌ Signed agreements (use secure document storage)
❌ Confidential negotiations
❌ Attorney-client privileged communications
❌ Customer contracts (use CRM/contract management)

---

## Updating Templates

1. Create new version, don't overwrite
2. Note changes in template header
3. Get legal review for substantive changes
4. Archive old version with date

---

## Disclaimer

Templates in this folder are for reference only. They do not constitute legal advice. Consult qualified legal counsel before using any template for actual agreements.
